About Forza

Forza is a volunteer platform that collects and displays the most important university and high school scholarships in one place. Our goal is to provide a reliable and constantly updated source that includes all the details students need, such as admission requirements, types of funding, application deadlines, and registration steps. Our platform is designed to facilitate students’ access to educational opportunities that help them build a better future.

Platform features

Trusted content

We rely on official sources to ensure the accuracy of the information that reaches the student
